About The Event

After 6 years, PRAYAS is back—bigger, bolder, and more fun than ever! 🚀 We've lined up thrilling competitions just. for. you!

Organized by Government Polytechnic, Amravati, PRAYAS 2K25 is a state-level technical fest where innovation meets competition. Whether you're a coding maniac, a quiz whiz, or a circuit-solving pro, there's something for everyone.

The Legacy Returns! PRAYAS has always been a hub of talent, innovation, and technical brilliance. After 2019, it was put on hold due to the pandemic. But now, it's back!

Are you ready to take on the challenge? Be there, make your PRAYAS, and claim your victory! 🔥
